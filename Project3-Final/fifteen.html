Fifteen Puzzle Game

×

The 15 puzzle (also called Gem Puzzle, Boss Puzzle, Game of Fifteen, Mystic Square and many others) is a sliding puzzle that consists of a frame of numbered square tiles in random order with one tile missing. The game is by default in 4X4 grids, but other sizes can be selected. The goal of the puzzle is to place tiles in order by making sliding moves that use the empty space. Once the puzzle is solved, you will get an alert and the page will change color.
